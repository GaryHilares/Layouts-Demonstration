Fluid layout

What is a fluid layout?

A fluid layout is the layout which resizes itself depending on the viewport and other variables.

How to create it with CSS?

Write the widths of your elements using percentages in CSS.

Advantages of a fluid layout

  • Whole content can be seen.
  • There is no extra whitespace to the sides on big viewports.

Disadvantages of a fluid layout

  • It may be harder to wrap your mind around percentages.
  • The size of some items may be unpredictable.
